🐰 Bunz AI — Agentic Trading Companion for Bitget

Overview

Bunz AI is an intelligent, gamified trading companion built for the Bitget ecosystem.

Instead of interacting with a traditional trading dashboard, users interact with a living AI Rabbit ("Bunz") that observes markets, analyzes risk, rewards learning, and guides traders through a complete decision-making process.

The goal is to transform crypto trading from a passive dashboard experience into an engaging AI-powered companion experience.

---

Problem

Most trading platforms provide charts, numbers, and execution tools, but they do not actively help users understand markets, improve decision-making, or develop better trading habits.

New traders often struggle with:

- Information overload
- Emotional trading
- Poor risk management
- Lack of feedback and learning systems

---

Solution

Bunz AI combines:

- Agentic AI
- Real-time market intelligence
- Risk management
- Gamification

into a single trading companion experience.

Users can talk to Bunz naturally, analyze markets, track progress, unlock achievements, and improve their trading behavior over time.

---

Agentic Trading Loop

Bunz AI follows a complete trading intelligence cycle:

1. Perception

The system continuously observes market conditions using:

- Live Bitget Market Data
- BTCUSDT Spot Market Information
- Future sentiment/news integrations

The AI Rabbit acts as the observation layer.

2. Decision

Bunz analyzes incoming market data and generates:

- Bullish/Bearish market bias
- Confidence scores
- Market summaries
- Trading suggestions

This transforms raw market data into actionable intelligence.

3. Execution

Current MVP:

- Paper-trading style recommendations
- Signal generation

Future:

- Bitget API order execution
- Trading automation
- Strategy deployment

4. Risk Management

Every decision passes through a risk layer:

- Risk scoring
- Position sizing concepts
- User guidance
- Behavioral coaching

This ensures responsible trading practices.

Live Bitget Integration

The MVP is connected to real Bitget market data.

Current implementation:

- BTCUSDT Spot Market Analysis
- Live price retrieval
- Real-time market trend detection
- Dynamic AI-generated responses

When the user presses "Analyze Market", Bunz fetches live market information from Bitget and generates a market summary.

This demonstrates a real and runnable integration instead of static mock data.
